Chat Usage Rules

Welcome to our chat! Please follow these rules to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone:

  • Respectful Behavior: Users must maintain polite and respectful communication. Offensive, abusive, or inappropriate language is strictly prohibited.
  • No Spamming: Repeated messages, irrelevant content, or advertisements that disrupt the chat are not allowed.
  • Privacy Respect: Do not share personal information of other users without their consent. This includes personal data, addresses, phone numbers, or any sensitive information.
  • Prohibited Illegal Content: Any content promoting illegal activities, violence, or hate speech is strictly forbidden and may result in the user being banned from the chat.
  • Registration and Access: Users must be registered and authenticated to join the chat. Login credentials must be kept secure.
  • Moderation: The chat is monitored to ensure rule compliance. Moderators have the right to warn or remove users who violate the rules.
  • Maintain Security: Users must follow basic security principles, avoiding malicious use of the chat, such as sharing harmful files or links.
  • Content Responsibility: Each user is responsible for the content they post. If any rule violations are observed, users are encouraged to report them to the moderators.
Thank you for being a part of our community!
